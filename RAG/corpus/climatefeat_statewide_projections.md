# ClimateFEAT Statewide Peak Demand Projections

## Overview

Statewide projections from the ClimateFEAT transformer model across CMIP6 climate ensembles under two SSP scenarios. Peak demand is defined as the top 1% of predicted daily statewide demand (MWh) per year. Values represent the mean of top-1% days, averaged across ensemble members, with 10th and 90th percentile bounds showing ensemble spread.
