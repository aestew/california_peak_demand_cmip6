# ClimateFEAT TAC Area Peak Demand Projections

## Overview

County-level ClimateFEAT predictions rolled up to CAISO Transmission Access Charge (TAC) areas using population-weighted county splits. Split counties are allocated proportionally (e.g., Kern County: 55% PG&E, 45% SCE). Non-CAISO territories (LADWP, SMUD, IID) are shown separately.

Peak demand is defined as the mean of top-1% daily TAC-area demand per year, averaged across ensemble members.
